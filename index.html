WHAT IS OQUAKE

Built for the Arena. Connected to Everything.

OQUAKE is a heavily modified build of vkQuake — the premier Vulkan-based Quake port — reimagined as a living node in the OASIS Omniverse. Every player session is anchored to a shared, sovereign identity layer: your OASIS Avatar carries your stats, inventory, karma and NFT achievements across every game in the ecosystem.

Integration runs deep via OGEngine and the WEB5 STAR API. Weapons, armour and powerups aren't just spawned procedurally — they're physically anchored to real-world GPS coordinates in parks and public spaces. You must go there to collect them.

Items collected in the real world persist in your shared OASIS Inventory and can be used across multiple games in the Omniverse, traded as NFTs or carried into cross-game quests managed by OGEngine. When you frag an opponent, karma flows. When you complete a quest, an NFT trophy is minted.

The Vulkan render path (via vkQuake) ensures buttery-smooth framerates even at extreme resolutions, giving competitive players a genuine hardware advantage while the OASIS layer keeps every match connected, recorded and rewarded.

THE NEVER-ENDING STORY

Epic Cross-Game Quest Line

The rifts don't stop at Doom. Strange portals tear open in Quake's arenas — Doom imps stumbling through from Mars, Quake shamblers bleeding into UAC corridors. Keys found in ODOOM unlock doors here. Powerups discovered in Our World change the tide of battle. The quest line spans every game in the OASIS Omniverse — it starts in ODOOM, and OQuake is where it tears open into something far bigger.

Just like the original DOOM Episode 1 launched as shareware — free to play and free to share — Chapter I of the cross-game quest line will be completely free. It starts in ODOOM, passes through OQuake, loops back to Our World, and returns to ODOOM to close. Coming Q3/Q4 2026 — watch this space.

⬡ ALIEN ARTEFACT RECOVERED · CLASSIFICATION: UNKNOWN

Deep within Quake's dimensions — beyond the Slipgate, beyond the Elder World — Champions discover an artefact unlike anything in the known arsenal: the Phase Shift Device. Of alien origin, its purpose was initially unclear. What is now known: it is the key to crossing between worlds, dimensions, and universes. It is what makes the OASIS Omniverse possible.

In the real world, the PSD is your phone running Our World. The same device you hold in your hand is, within the lore, the most powerful piece of technology in the multiverse. The immersion never breaks.
